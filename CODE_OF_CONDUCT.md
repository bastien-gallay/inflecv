# Code of Conduct

We adopt the [Contributor Covenant version 2.1][cc] verbatim as our
Code of Conduct. Read it in full at the link below; it covers
expected behaviour, unacceptable behaviour, scope, enforcement
responsibilities, and reporting.

[cc]: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/

## Reporting

Email the maintainer at the address listed in
[`pyproject.toml`](pyproject.toml). Reports are handled privately and
in good faith. No public escalation without giving the maintainer a
chance to act first.

## Enforcement

The maintainer decides on a case-by-case basis, following the
Contributor Covenant's "Enforcement Guidelines" (correction → warning →
temporary ban → permanent ban). Sustained or severe violations bypass
intermediate steps.
